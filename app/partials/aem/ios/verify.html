Your device should be displayed in the device list AEM was successfully implemented. Select your device. On the right you should see a tabbed panel showing information about the device you selected. The Dashboard tab will display information about your device, such as location, battery status, network information, app uptime, app version and SDK version, etc. The Settings tab is where you will be able to remotely manage the ASM (App Settings Management) features of the SDK once it is implemented into your app. From the Actions drop-down above the device list you can request a screen shot or device logs. Screenshots and Logs are displayed in the Details tab. If you’re able to see all we’ve mentioned, your device is enabled.
