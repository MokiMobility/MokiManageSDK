Updating Your Schema

When you make changes to your SettingsSchema.json file, update the version at the beginning of the SettingsSchema so that the API set knows you’ve made a change. Increment the version attribute by 1, as it is just an integer.

	"Version": 2,

This change will notify the MokiManage.com console to make a schema change. If you make a schema change, and don’t increment your version number, your changes will be ignored.
