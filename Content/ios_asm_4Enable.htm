Enabling ASM

In QuickStart: AEM for iOS, the example code instructs you to set enableASM: to NO when you call initializeWithApiKey: inside of the app delegate's didFinishLaunchingWithOptions: method. In order to enable ASM, you need to ensure your SettingsSchema.json is included in your Xcode project and change the value of enableASM: to YES.

NSError *error;

    [[MokiManage sharedManager] initializeWithApiKey:API_KEY
                                    launchingOptions:launchOptions
                                           enableASM:YES
                                           enableAEM:YES
                                 asmSettingsFileName:nil
                                               error:&error];
    
    [[MokiManage sharedManager] setDelegate:self];
